About Us

Who We Are &
What We Stand For

The IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter at General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University is one of Sri Lanka's most active and vibrant student technology organizations. Founded in 2016, we are a community of passionate students united by our love for computing, technology, and innovation.

As an official affiliate of the world's premier organization for computing professionals, we bring global-standard resources, knowledge, and opportunities directly to our members at KDU.

  • Officially affiliated with the IEEE Computer Society — the world's leading tech organization.
  • Hosting technical workshops, hackathons, and industry talks year-round.
  • Connecting students with industry professionals and global IEEE networks.
  • Fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership in technology.
  • Open to all students at KDU with a passion for computing and technology.

Our Journey

Chapter Milestones

A proud history of achievements, growth, and impact since our founding in 2016.

2016

Chapter Founded

The IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter at KDU was officially established, becoming one of the first IEEE CS chapters in the region.

2018

First IEEEXtreme Participation

KDU teams participated in IEEEXtreme for the first time, marking our entry into the global competitive programming arena.

2020

Digital Transition & Growth

Successfully pivoted all chapter activities online during the pandemic, expanding membership reach and conducting virtual workshops and webinars.

2022

HackBoost — Flagship Hackathon Launched

The inaugural HackBoost hackathon was conducted, attracting 150+ participants from across Sri Lanka and establishing KDU as a key tech event destination.

2024

Ideate 360° & Industry Programs

Launched the transformative Ideate 360° entrepreneurship workshop and expanded industry collaboration programs, engaging 250+ students in career-focused activities.

2025

New Board & Chapter Rebrand

The 2025/26 board officially inducted, launching a new website, expanded programs, and an exciting new era of innovation and growth for IEEE CS KDU.
